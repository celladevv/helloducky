Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026

Ducky is an independent project. This page explains what happens if you join the waitlist. In short: the only thing collected is your email address, only if you choose to give it, and only to tell you when Ducky launches.

What is collected

Your email address, and only that, when you submit the waitlist form. Nothing else is requested or required.

Why

Solely to notify you once, when Ducky is publicly available to download. Your email is not used for anything else and is never sold or shared.

Who processes it

The waitlist form is handled by Tally, which stores submissions on your behalf. The website itself uses Cloudflare Web Analytics, which is cookieless and does not collect personal data.

How long it is kept

Until Ducky launches and the notification is sent, or until you ask to be removed, whichever comes first.

Your rights

You can ask to see or delete your email at any time. Just email cella.productjourney@gmail.com and it will be removed.

Contact

Questions about this policy: cella.productjourney@gmail.com
